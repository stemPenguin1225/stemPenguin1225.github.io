The AllergenCam Project is created!


Posted by Henry Gifford | April 2026


We're excited to share that AllergenCam is officially in the works — and we'd love for you to be among the first to know when it launches.

AllergenCam was built around a simple belief: knowing what's in your food shouldn't be stressful. Point your camera at any food label or ingredient list and get instant, clear allergen alerts — no guesswork, no anxiety.

We're working hard to get it into your hands, and we're almost there.

If you or someone you love manages a food allergy, check the blog often or get on our mailing list! (Coming soon)

AllergenCam — eat with confidence.
